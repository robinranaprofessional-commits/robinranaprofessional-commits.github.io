Our Story

45 Years of Heritage & Community Connection

A Legacy of Flavour

Founded over 45 years ago, Señor Froggy has become a Kamloops institution. Built on family recipes, consistency, and a deep connection to the community, the restaurant continues to serve authentic Mexican cuisine with pride.

What started as a small family venture has grown into a beloved local landmark, serving generations of Kamloops residents and visitors. Our commitment to quality ingredients, traditional preparation methods, and warm hospitality has remained unchanged throughout the decades.

Family Values & Community

At Señor Froggy, we believe that great food brings people together. Our restaurant is more than just a place to eat — it's a gathering spot where families celebrate, friends reconnect, and the community comes together.

We take pride in being locally owned and operated, supporting local suppliers whenever possible, and giving back to the community that has supported us for over four decades.
